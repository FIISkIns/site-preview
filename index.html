Learn a new skill!

SkIns was created for educational purposes to offer users free tutorials in various fields. Our goal is to make learning fun and to help people learn new skills.

Origami

Origami involves the creation of two-dimensional and three-dimensional paper objects, usually enirely by folding. This course will take you through the basics of origami and will also provide you with tutorials for creating models such as animals, birds, geometric shapes and decorative objects.

Survival

Strengthen your character and learn how to react in any situation! Survival skills training will help you overcome the fear of confronting the unknown and make the best decisions in the most unexpected events. Here you will discover elementary notions of survival in the wild, but also in the urban jungle.

Self-defence

Self-defence is the use of force to protect yourself against someone who is attacking you. In this course you will learn the importance of self-defence, why you should learn it and how to perform basic self-defence methods, such as escaping headlocks, defence against knives, leg grab counters and others.
